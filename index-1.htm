MP4 is the only video file format available. It is supported by all major media players. You can be sure that your video will be played on any device with an installed media player.

Where are the files stored on my device?

By default, the downloaded files are stored in a default browser folder called Downloads. This folder is usually stored in Documents folder on your desktop device, Windows, macOS, Linux and others. Press CTRL+J on Windows or CMD+J on macOS, to open the download history of your browser, there you can find the files you downloaded earlier.

To find this folder on your iPhone or Android, just tap the folder name on the search bar of your phone or tablet, please do not confuse the search bar of the phone with the search bar of the search engine.
